What is Meme Creator?

Meme Creator is a fun and interactive web app designed to let you create, edit, and share memes easily. You can upload your own images or choose from popular templates, add captions, apply filters, and save your creations.

Features

  • 🎨 Meme Editor with drag-and-drop text
  • 📂 Save and view your memes in a gallery
  • ✨ Apply filters like grayscale, sepia, and contrast
  • 🤖 Generate random AI captions
  • 🌙 Toggle Dark Mode for a stylish look

Our Goal

The goal of Meme Creator is to make meme-making simple, creative, and enjoyable for everyone — whether you’re a student, programmer, or just someone who loves sharing laughs.
